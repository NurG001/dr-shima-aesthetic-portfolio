Beyond Wrinkles: A Holistic Approach to Anti-Aging

By Dr. Amina Arafat Shima

Aging is natural, but how we age is often a choice. In my clinic, we move beyond "fixing wrinkles" to "restoring health." True anti-aging is about maintaining skin function and elasticity over time.

When Should You Start?

The best time to start anti-aging care is in your mid-20s. This is when collagen production begins to slow down. Prevention is always easier, cheaper, and more effective than correction.

The Pillars of Anti-Aging

1. Sun Protection (SPF)

80% of visible aging is caused by UV rays. Daily sunscreen is the most powerful anti-aging tool you own.

2. Retinoids

Vitamin A derivatives speed up cell turnover, keeping skin fresh and reducing pore size.

3. In-Clinic Maintenance

Treatments like chemical peels, microneedling, and occasional laser sessions act like a "gym workout" for your skin cells, keeping them active and producing collagen.
